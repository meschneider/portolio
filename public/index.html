Recent Projects



iCompliment

During the beginning of 2017 I worked as a UX Design Researcher with a group of three other students in order to create a novel social experience. Our solution: iCompliment, a location-based application which allows users to spread and receive free love through anonymous messages.

Dine With Me

During the spring of 2017 I worked as a Developer and UX Designer with a group of two other students to create a location-based application. Our solution was Dine With Me, an application which allows users to connect with those around them in order to find someone to eat with.

Moments

In the fall of 2016 I worked as a UX Design Researcher and Developer with a group of two other students. Our task was to create an application based on the theme of exploration. We created Moments, an application which allowed users to document their experiences exploring the world around them.
